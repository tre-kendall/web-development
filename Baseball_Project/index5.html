Best of the Best:

An "All-Star" can be categorized as a player who excels above others. A term that can apply to all sports, typically all-stars are known for their top performance throughout each season consistantly. Some all-stars even have the capability to completely turn around a season/game for their entire team! With other possible names such as "fan favorites" and "hall of famers", these individuals are often praised for their excellent statistics, plays, and presence in the league. In this section of the website I'd like to share a couple players who fit these descriptions with some compilations of their achievements!
